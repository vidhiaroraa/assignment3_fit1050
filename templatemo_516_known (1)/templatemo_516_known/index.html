from home kitchen to bakery

from home kitchen to bakery 4me gluten free owner, Yinon, has over 20 years experience in the baking industry. For over a decade he ran a successful gluten free baking business abroad, exporting his goods worldwide.

Our Beliefs

our products are made in a 100% gluten and nut free environment. We offer food alternative solutions to; Allergy sufferers, including Coeliac disease sufferers ,Nut allergy sufferers ,Lactose allergy sufferers ,Customers who have specific dietary requirements
